Colour palette

Primary

The primary colour of the website is used to provide an accenting splash of colour or to draw attention. It should be used sparingly so as to retain its importance and not pollute the dark theme.

Font & base

All font is pure white by default, the base colour is used as the page background, the basis for the elevation gradient, and as a font colour where white does not provide a high enough contrast. The base colour was created by overlaying the primary colour on top of Dark Grey (#121212) at 8% opacity.

Breakpoints

This website is designed to be mobile first with additional breakpoints at the page widths 37.5rem, 56.25rem, 75rem and 112.5rem. These widths were chosen as they fall between the most commonly used screen sizes. Elevation

Elevation is expressed by adjusting the background colour of elements, a lighter background appears closer to the viewer and so in front of darker elements. The gradient was created by overlaying white on the base colour at progressively higher opacities.

Typography

Montserrat

500 weight

Montserrat is used once per page in the heading, where its commanding presence has the greatest impact.

"The ships hung in the sky in much the same way that bricks don't."
Google fonts

Jost

400 & 600 weight

Less impactful that Montserrat, Jost is used for all other text, predominantly in 400 weight but 600 is used where greater emphasis is required, for example, in buttons.
